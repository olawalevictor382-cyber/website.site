The House

Built on a needle,
a mirror, and a lot
of patience.

Vicky Made started in a single fitting room with one mirror and one client at a time. Every piece was cut to a real body, not a size chart — and more than a decade later, that hasn't changed.

Today the atelier designs suits, occasion wear, and ready-to-wear pieces for men who want clothing built to last, not just to photograph well. Each garment passes through the same hands, from the first sketch to the final stitch.

01

Craftsmanship

Every seam is finished by hand, inside and out — no shortcuts on what you can't see.

02

Fit

Patterns are drafted to your own measurements, not adjusted from a stock size.

03

Longevity

Construction built to be worn, altered, and worn again for years to come.

What We Build

Seven ways into the atelier.

Every project starts as a conversation about occasion, silhouette, and fabric — then becomes a pattern with your name on it.

01 Bespoke Tailoring Suits, coats, and separates drafted from your own personal pattern block.
02 Wedding & Occasion Suits Groom's and groomsmen's suits, designed and fitted around your exact day.
03 Black-Tie & Formalwear Dinner jackets and formal suiting built for the room you're about to walk into.
04 Ready-to-Wear Collections Seasonal pieces cut in small runs and finished entirely by hand.
05 Alterations & Fittings Structural alterations and fit corrections for garments you already own.
06 Fashion Consultations One-on-one sessions on silhouette, fabric, and construction choices.
07 Custom Designs A garment built from a sketch, a reference image, or a description.
